Recording-state differentiation options (Claude's discretion — pick ONE; RESEARCH §"Pattern 3" recommends "filled vs outline"):
A) Filled vs outline glyph: idle = U+25CB (white circle outline), recording = U+25CF (black circle filled). Same colour. Most visually distinct.
B) Two shades: idle = "#B388EB", recording = "#7E54C9" (deeper purple). Single glyph, two colours.
C) Alpha shift: idle = lavender at alpha 0.5, recording = lavender at alpha 1.0. Subtle.

Recommended: A. Simplest, most visually clear, no font dependency.

Icon SVG spec (D-10):
- viewBox + width + height = 0 0 256 256 (explicit dimensions for sips reliability — RESEARCH Pitfall 6)
- One <rect> filling the canvas with lavender fill (#B388EB)
